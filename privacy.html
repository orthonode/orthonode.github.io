Privacy Policy

Orthonode Systems  ·  Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Orthonode Systems ("we," "us," or "Orthonode") operates the website at orthonode.xyz. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit the Site, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Short version: We do not directly collect personal data. The Site is static HTML served via GitHub Pages. Standard web infrastructure (GitHub Pages CDN, Google Fonts, Cloudflare) may collect web access logs as described below.

Information We Collect

GitHub Pages (Hosting)

This Site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard web access logs including IP addresses, browser type, referring URLs, and page visit timestamps. This data is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Google Fonts (CDN)

This Site loads fonts from Google Fonts CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may log font requests including IP address and browser information. This is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare Turnstile (Bot Protection)

Certain pages on this Site use Cloudflare Turnstile to verify that visitors are human. Turnstile does not use behavioral tracking cookies or invasive fingerprinting. It operates by analyzing interaction patterns to distinguish humans from bots. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not run analytics services on this Site (no Google Analytics, no tracking pixels)
  • We do not use advertising networks or ad tracking
  • We do not set first-party cookies
  • We do not collect form submissions, email addresses, or any user-submitted data through the Site
  • We do not store wallet addresses or blockchain identifiers from Site visitors

Email Contact

If you contact us at infrastructure@orthonode.xyz, we will receive your email address and the content of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Blockchain Interactions

Orthonode Systems protocol smart contracts are deployed on public blockchains (Arbitrum Sepolia, TON Testnet, Solana Devnet). All on-chain transactions are public and permanently recorded on the respective blockchain. Blockchain data is not controlled by Orthonode Systems — we cannot modify, delete, or restrict access to on-chain records.

Wallet addresses that interact with Orthonode contracts are visible in the public transaction history of those contracts.

Third-Party Links

The Site contains links to external websites, block explorers, GitHub repositories, and other third-party resources. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the Site, contact us and we will address the situation promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
