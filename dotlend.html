ECOSYSTEM_GAP

Polkadot has $330M in HOLLAR and vDOT at 76% utilization on Hydration — supply cap hit, demand proven. Yet there are zero native lending markets on Polkadot Hub.

→ vDOT supply cap hit

Every dollar of vDOT collateral sitting idle on Hydration is a failure of the ecosystem. vDOT earns ~15% APY staking yield — holders deserve to borrow against it without selling. DotLend fixes this.

WHY_NOT_HYDRATION

Hydration is an AMM. DotLend is a CDP engine.

Hydration lets you supply to liquidity pools. It cannot give you a collateralized debt position — there is no borrow-against-collateral mechanism. These are complementary, not competing protocols.

DotLend is a liquidity source for Hydration: deposit vDOT → borrow HOLLAR → deploy HOLLAR into Hydration pools. Victor Xu (Bifrost) confirmed vDOT lending is the #1 requested feature from the community.

// PROTOCOL_MECHANICS : CDP_DESIGN

COLLATERALIZED_DEBT_POSITION ✓ LIVE ON TESTNET POLKADOT SOLIDITY 2026

Deposit vDOT. Borrow HOLLAR. Earn staking yield while borrowing.

DotLend is a non-custodial money market protocol. Users deposit vDOT (Bifrost's liquid staking token) as collateral and borrow HOLLAR (Hydration's over-collateralized stablecoin) at up to 70% LTV — while continuing to earn Bifrost staking yield (~15% APY) on their deposited assets.

Interest accrues lazily via block.timestamp — no cron, no keeper. Each user's debt is updated on-demand when they interact, keeping gas costs minimal and accounting trustless.

Positions become liquidatable when health factor drops below 1.0. A 5% liquidation bonus incentivizes external liquidators to keep the protocol solvent.

The Noir circuit (UltraHonk proving system) constrains sum(collateral_values) > sum(debt_amounts) with aggregate totals as public inputs and per-user positions as private witnesses. The circuit compiles cleanly and generates valid proofs off-chain.

Every 30 minutes a Railway worker reads all active positions, computes collateral and debt totals, and calls SolvencyGateway.publishSolvencyProof(). A SolvencyProven event is emitted on-chain and visible on Blockscout.
