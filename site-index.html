Orthonode Systems builds trust primitives for decentralized systems — hardware identity anchoring on Arbitrum (SHA), agent trust on TON (TON-SHA), chain-agnostic attestation (OAP), and zero-trust gateway infrastructure (Nexus Protocol). Type B projects explore DePIN governance (TIX-DAO) and AI trust scoring (INVARIANT).
Orthonode Systems is based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. Founded by Arhant Barmate. Reach us at infrastructure@orthonode.xyz or via @OrthonodeSys on Twitter.
Orthonode currently operates on Arbitrum (SHA Protocol — Sepolia testnet), TON (TON-SHA — testnet), and Solana (TIX-DAO — devnet). OAP and Nexus Protocol are chain-agnostic infrastructure layers designed to operate across any EVM or non-EVM chain.
