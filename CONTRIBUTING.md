Thank you for your interest in Orthonode Systems. Please note that this repository is the official gateway for **Orthonode Infrastructure Labs** and is currently maintained as a **closed-contribution** project.

### Pull Requests (PRs)
We do not accept public Pull Requests for this repository. Any PRs opened by external accounts will be closed without review to maintain the integrity of our deployment pipeline and legal compliance.

### Bug Reports & Feedback
If you notice a technical error in the console or documentation:
1. Open a **GitHub Issue** with the label `bug`.
2. Provide a clear description of the issue.
3. For architectural feedback or partnership inquiries regarding the **Nexus Protocol (v1.4.0)**, please contact us at `infrastructure@orthonode.xyz`.

### Research & Development
Development of the **Orthonode Assurance Platform (OAP)** and the **Nexus Protocol** occurs in restricted environments. If you are an engineer interested in the kinematic attestation stack, we recommend following our updates on [X (@OrthonodeSys)](https://x.com/OrthonodeSys).
