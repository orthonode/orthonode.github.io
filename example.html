Core Doctrine

Six laws
no system
can override.

Not design principles. Axioms. Every component of Orthonode infrastructure derives from them — and every attack we block traces to a violation of one.

  • 01
    Physics is invariant.
    Reality cannot be overridden by software.
  • 02
    Identity must be device-bound.
    Silicon cannot be cloned. Software can.
  • 03
    Trust must be lifecycle-governed.
    Devices degrade. Trust models must adapt.
  • 04
    Integrity must be append-only.
    History cannot be rewritten.
  • 05
    Redemption must be bounded.
    Unbounded reward loops create unbounded fraud.
  • 06
    Verification must be deterministic.
    Probabilistic trust is not trust.
