INVARIANT — Deterministic Trust Infrastructure for Bittensor

Every Bittensor subnet today trusts miner outputs. INVARIANT makes them provable. Three independently unfakeable layers — SHA hardware identity, execution receipts, OAP lifecycle scoring — stacked into a cryptographically-verified NTS (INVARIANT Trust Score) that permanently weights TAO emissions.

The emission formula: weight = output_quality × (NTS/100) × freshness. A miner with NTS 40 and a perfect output still earns 40% of what an honest miner earns. Past behavior permanently shapes future earnings.
