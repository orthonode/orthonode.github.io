FLAGSHIP_PROTOCOL

SHA (Stylus Hardware Anchor)

Production-grade Rust/WASM attestation protocol that cryptographically binds physical silicon identities (ESP32 eFuses) to Arbitrum smart contracts. Eliminates Sybil attacks in DePIN ecosystems through immutable hardware fingerprinting and on-chain verification.

Architecture: Four-stage verification pipeline (identity gate, firmware governance, replay protection, cryptographic proof) executing in 45,000 gas via Stylus WASM optimization.

RESEARCH_MODULE

OAP (Orthonode Assurance Platform)

Physics-based telemetry validation engine. Cross-references GPS trajectories against accelerometer noise-floor signatures to distinguish genuine physical activity from emulated data streams. Real-world friction patterns are impossible to replicate in software.

EXPERIMENTAL_ARCH

Nexus Protocol

Zero-trust fabric for edge compute. Enforces fail-closed execution at hardware gate before consensus layer ingestion. Transport-agnostic deterministic verification with local attestation enforcement.
